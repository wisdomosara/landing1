Features

Clear & Simple User Interface

Easy to navigate and interact with. It makes it easy for you to know what to do at every point in the app. Unnecessary elements are avoided. The app is straight-forward to make the experience a memorable one for you.

Silent mode based on set locations

You can set locations where on appearance in those locations, your phone automatically switches to silent mode. After leaving the location, it switches back to Ringer mode.

Phone auto-silent mode using noise analysis

Office, meetings, interviews, corporate events and religious environements often have surrounding noise levels of less than 100dB. Using noise analysis, the phone detects your surrounding noise level and switches sutomatically to silent mode

Sync calendar events with app using personal emails

This app allows you to sync your Google, Yahoo and Outlook emails with the app and automatically, your calendar events are synced with timed silent mode.
